ABOUT ARTISTS ASPIRATIONS ASKUS

-----TWOTHOUSAND RECORDS is a speculative record company that revolves around the idea that all music should be created to be shared, not just to make a profit. It comprises 10 unique artists who have all signed non-binding contracts that allow them the resources and support they need to create their magnum opus while we stick to what we do best, sharing your music with the world.
Our artists have signed on with no expectation to release music. That's right, our main goal isn’t to release work but to give the support and resources needed to create so that we can grow with our artists and be there when they want to release. We strive to work with our artists to produce work that will destroy boundaries, not define them. We also partner with the Working Artists Union to ensure that our artists are comfortable where they are so that we can focus on what we do best, sharing your music worldwide.
